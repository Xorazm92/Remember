Benefits for Trucking companies

INDUSTRY EXPERTS AND LEGISLATIVE REPRESENTATION

TLA advances the interests of trucking companies operating in Texas through regulatory and legislative advocacy at the local, state and national levels. TLA TruckPAC is an essential tool and staff has decades of advocacy prowess - our experts are just a phone call away.

ANNUAL CONFERENCE

Each summer, trucking executives from around the state gather for the TXTA Annual Conference, featuring a unique opportunity for networking with industry peers, outstanding educational sessions and renowned speakers.

TLA UNIVERSITY

TLA University offers the best online repository of trucking knowledge anywhere. Our trainers videos cover the entire range of state and federal regulations, safety practices, fleet management topics and much more. TLA works closely with the Texas Department of Motor Vehicles (TxDMV), Texas Department of Public Safety (TxDOT) and Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) to ensure our training and testing materials are always up to date. TLA members have exclusive access to this expertise and every webinar we create. A test must be passed to receive a certificate of completion.
